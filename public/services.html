Road Freight

As the backbone of our operations, our road freight service offers unparalleled connectivity across Moldova and Europe. We manage full truckload (FTL) and less-than-truckload (LTL) shipments, ensuring your goods are transported efficiently and on schedule, whether for domestic distribution or international transit.

Air Freight

For time-critical shipments, ProrucTrans provides swift and secure air freight solutions. Leveraging our global partnerships, we connect Chișinău to major international air hubs, managing all logistics for a seamless, door-to-door experience for your high-value and perishable goods.
